What We Do ?

  • Hair transplantation in Turkey
    Advanced hair transplantation, with a lifetime guarantee, with the latest medical technologies, and the largest number of follicles in the world.
  • Dental treatment and implants
    Dental and gum treatment, dental implants at competitive prices; We also use all kinds of ceramic lenses and veneers (veneers - luminaries - IMEX - Empers) to treat deformities and fractures of teeth.
  • Obesity treatment and obesity surgery
    Advanced treatment programs: gastric balloon, sleeve gastrectomy, gastric banding, gastric botox, gastric bypass . You will recover your life with easy and safe procedures, with continuous psychological support before, during and after the treatment journey.
  • Vision correction operations
    We have the latest vision correction and ophthalmology technologies such as: LASIK, Femotolasek, Femto Smile or Smile Lasik. The most appropriate technique for each case is determined by the attending physician.
  • Orthopedic and spine surgery
    Joints, prosthetics, spine surgery, scoliosis, bone tumors, sports injuries such as cruciate ligament tears, back pain, and other highly specialized specialties and techniques for all orthopedic problems.
  • Heart surgeries
    All open-heart robotic procedures, complex cardiovascular surgeries, as well as minimally invasive heart surgeries, and all subspecialties of the heart of children and adults.
  • Diabetes treatment
    Treatment of type 2 diabetes, by means of a metabolic surgery that is performed in only 8 centers in the world, one of which is in Turkey for your medical treatment
